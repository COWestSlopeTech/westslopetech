{% include section-header.html title="Our Mission" %}

The Roaring Fork Technologists are committed to fostering technological solutions within the community for the purpose of technical business education, economic stimulus, and securing a lifestyle we celebrate for years to come.

{% include section-header.html title="What We Do" %}

We are a group of volunteers who are on a mission to support our community. Outside of our day jobs we want to help in what capacity we can. This includes regular meetups to work on projects together as well as offering support to local entrepeneurs and schools.
